Research

I am mainly interested in the neural mechanisms of attention, and how attention shapes visual perception. Currently I am a PhD student in the Cognition and Plasticity lab of Heleen Slagter, funded by an NWO Research Talent grant. My PhD project focuses on improving various forms of attention (but mostly spatial attention) through transcranial Direct Current Stimulation (tDCS). I also have extensive experience with EEG, as well as eye tracking and psychophysics. I support open science principles and try to make my work as transparent and reproducible as possible.
